Recipe #1

  • Prep Time: 5 mins
  • Cook Time: 20 mins
  • Calories 667 kcal
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity:

1. 12 ounces penne pasta

2. 3 tablespoons butter

3. 3 cups mozzarella shreads

4. 1 teasoppn garlic powder

5. 1 1/2 cups milk

6. 1 teaspoon salt

7. 1/3 teaspoon pepper

8. 1 cuo fresh spinach leaves

Prepartion method:

Step 1: Cook the pasta.

Step 2: Melt the butter in a large pot. Add the cheese spreads,salt,pepper,garlic powder, and milk. Cook over medium heat, stirring often.

Step 3: Once the cheese is melted and the sauce is smooth, add the spinach. Stir until the spinach wilts.

Step 4: Remove the pan from the heat and blend the sauce using an immersion blender.

Step 5: Add the cooked pasta to cheesy sauce. Stir well and Serve immediately
